 3. **FFT Peak Identification:** Configure the FFT window for high frequency resolution (\Delta f < 0.1\text{ Hz}). Look for persistent, non-random peaks at the 18.08 Hz interval.
 4. **Dielectric Loading:** If the signal is weak, introduce ferrite-core materials or high-permittivity dielectric substrates to the sensor housing. This increases the "capture cross-section" of the device, effectively treating the planetary grid as a tunable instrument.
